How to Spot Fake News

Check the Source

Verify the credibility of the news organization and author. Look for established, reputable sources.

Read Beyond the Headline

Sensational headlines can be misleading. Read the full article to understand the context.

Verify with Other Sources

Cross-check the information with multiple reliable news outlets to confirm its accuracy.

Watch for Emotional Language

Fake news often uses strong emotional language to provoke reactions rather than inform.
